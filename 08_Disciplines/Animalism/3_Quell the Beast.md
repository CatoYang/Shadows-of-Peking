# Quell the Beast (Animalism Level 3)

**Cost:** 1 Rouse Check
**Dice Pool:** Charisma + Animalism vs Stamina + Resolve
**Duration:** One scene

### System
Calm a frenzying vampire or completely pacify a mortal.
