# Cauldron of Blood (Blood Sorcery Level 5)

**Cost:** 1 Rouse Check
**Dice Pool:** Resolve + Blood Sorcery vs Stamina + Resolve
**Duration:** Instant

### System
Touch a victim and cause their blood to literally boil in their veins, inflicting catastrophic aggravated damage.
