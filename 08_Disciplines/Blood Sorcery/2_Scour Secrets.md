# Scour Secrets (Blood Sorcery Level 2)

**Cost:** 1 Rouse Check
**Dice Pool:** Intelligence + Blood Sorcery
**Duration:** Varies based on the scope of the search

### System
Introduced in the *Players Guide*. This power allows a Blood Sorcerer to magically investigate vast amounts of text, books, or documents to locate specific information in a matter of minutes. The vampire's blood washes over the objects and leaves a "telltale bloodstain" on the items investigated. On a success, the user intuitively locates and understands the specific information they were searching for within the documents. The difficulty ranges from 2 (a single letter in an office) to 5 or higher (encrypted or supernaturally concealed information in a vast library). The user cannot understand languages they do not already know.
