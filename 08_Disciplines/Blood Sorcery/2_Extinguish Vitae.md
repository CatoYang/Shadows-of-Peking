# Extinguish Vitae (Blood Sorcery Level 2)

**Cost:** 1 Rouse Check
**Dice Pool:** Intelligence + Blood Sorcery vs Stamina + Composure
**Duration:** Instant

### System
Force another vampire to lose a point of Hunger by destroying their vitae.
