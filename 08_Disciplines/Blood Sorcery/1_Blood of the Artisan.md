# Blood of the Artisan (Blood Sorcery Level 1)

**Cost:** 1 Rouse Check
**Dice Pool:** None
**Duration:** One scene

### System
The vampire can use their vitae to sculpt and manipulate inanimate matter (like clay, metal) with bare hands.
