# Sever the Blood Sympathy (Blood Sorcery Ritual Level 5)

**Cost:** 2 Rouse Checks
**Dice Pool:** Intelligence + Blood Sorcery
**Duration:** Permanent

### System
Completely sever the mystical connection between a vampire and their sire or childer, rendering them immune to tracking or sympathy powers.
