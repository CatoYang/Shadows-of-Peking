# Calix Secretus (Blood Sorcery Ritual Level 2)

**Cost:** 1 Rouse Check (+ blood stored)
**Dice Pool:** Intelligence + Blood Sorcery
**Duration:** Permanent until consumed

### System
Transform a small container into a mystical receptacle that can store vitae indefinitely without it spoiling, losing its properties, or causing blood bonds.
