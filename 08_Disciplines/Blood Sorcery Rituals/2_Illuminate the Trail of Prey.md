# Illuminate the Trail of Prey (Blood Sorcery Ritual Level 2)

**Cost:** 1 Rouse Check
**Dice Pool:** Intelligence + Blood Sorcery
**Duration:** One scene

### System
Causes the glowing footprints or tire tracks of a specific target to become visible to the caster.
