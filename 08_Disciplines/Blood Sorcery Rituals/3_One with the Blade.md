# One with the Blade (Blood Sorcery Ritual Level 3)

**Cost:** 1 Rouse Check
**Dice Pool:** Intelligence + Blood Sorcery
**Duration:** Permanent until stolen/destroyed

### System
Mystically bond with a melee weapon. The weapon never rusts or dulls. By anointing it with vitae, you gain a +2 dice bonus to attacks with it.
