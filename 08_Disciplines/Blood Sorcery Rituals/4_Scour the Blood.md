# Scour the Blood (Blood Sorcery Rituals Level 4)

**Cost:** 1 Rouse Check
**Dice Pool:** Intelligence + Blood Sorcery
**Duration:** Instant

### System
Remove Blood Bonds or other supernatural taints from a target's blood through a painful ritual.
