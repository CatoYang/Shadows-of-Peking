# Shaft of Belated Quiescence (Blood Sorcery Rituals Level 5)

**Cost:** 2 Rouse Checks
**Dice Pool:** Intelligence + Blood Sorcery
**Duration:** Permanent until used

### System
Carve a wooden stake that, when it pierces a victim, slowly burrows its way into their heart over time.
