# Deflection of Wooden Doom (Blood Sorcery Rituals Level 3)

**Cost:** 1 Rouse Check
**Dice Pool:** Intelligence + Blood Sorcery
**Duration:** Until triggered

### System
Causes the next wooden stake aimed at the vampire's heart to shatter.
