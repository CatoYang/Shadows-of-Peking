# Sheriff

The **Sheriff** is the Prince's chief enforcer, investigator, and executioner. They are the muscle of the court, tasked with maintaining physical order and hunting down those who break the Traditions or the Prince's laws.

**Responsibilities and Powers:**
- Investigating breaches of the Traditions (especially the Masquerade).
- Apprehending suspects and bringing them to the Prince.
- Enforcing order at court and in Elysium (at the Keeper's request).
- Often leading the charge during a Blood Hunt.
