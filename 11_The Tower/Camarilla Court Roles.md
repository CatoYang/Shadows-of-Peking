# Camarilla Court Roles

The Camarilla, the Ivory Tower, structures its domains with a strict hierarchy to enforce the Traditions and maintain order. The following are the standard positions found within a typical Camarilla court:

- [[Prince]]
- [[Seneschal]]
- [[Primogen]]
- [[Sheriff]]
- [[Hound]]
- [[Scourge]]
- [[Keeper of Elysium]]
- [[Harpy]]
- [[Herald]]
