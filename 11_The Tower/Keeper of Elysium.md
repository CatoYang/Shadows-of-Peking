# Keeper of Elysium

The **Keeper of Elysium** (or simply the Keeper) is responsible for managing and protecting the designated Elysium locations within a city—places where violence and Discipline use are strictly forbidden.

**Responsibilities and Powers:**
- Declaring and maintaining Elysium locations (often museums, theaters, or upscale clubs).
- Ensuring the strict rules of Elysium are not broken.
- Organizing court gatherings, parties, and artistic displays.
- Revoking Elysium privileges from those who misbehave.
- Punishing those who break the peace of Elysium (often calling upon the Sheriff for enforcement).
