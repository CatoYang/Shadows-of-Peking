# Scourge

The **Scourge** is a dark mirror to the Sheriff. While the Sheriff polices the recognized Kindred of the domain, the Scourge's primary duty is to deal with the unwanted, the unacknowledged, and the unauthorized. They patrol the borders of the city, both physical and societal.

**Responsibilities and Powers:**
- Hunting down and destroying Caitiff, thin-bloods, and unsanctioned childer.
- Destroying Sabbat infiltrators, Anarch troublemakers, or independent vampires who haven't introduced themselves to the Prince.
- Acting as a terror tactic to ensure fear of the Prince's displeasure.
- Often granted the right to destroy outcasts on sight without trial.
