# Hound

A **Hound** is a deputy, assistant, or subordinate to the Sheriff. While the Sheriff oversees the general security of the domain, the Hounds are the boots on the ground who do the tracking, patrolling, and rough work. 

**Responsibilities and Powers:**
- Assisting the Sheriff in investigations and apprehensions.
- Patrolling the domain to monitor Kindred activities.
- Gathering information from the streets.
- Executing violence or intimidation on behalf of the Sheriff or the Prince.
