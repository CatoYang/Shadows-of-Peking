# Primogen

The **Primogen** (or Primogen Council) are the recognized representatives of the Camarilla clans within a city. Collectively, they act as an advisory board to the Prince, but in practice, they are often a powerful legislature that can challenge the Prince's authority.

**Responsibilities and Powers:**
- Representing the interests of their specific Clan in the city.
- Advising the Prince on matters of policy and law.
- Serving as a check and balance against a tyrannical Prince.
- Often determining the internal structure and leadership of their own Clan within the domain.
