# Prince

The **Prince** is the undisputed ruler of a Camarilla domain, usually a single city. While nominally holding power due to their age, lineage, or martial prowess, a successful Prince maintains their Praxis (the right to rule) through a delicate balance of politics, favors, fear, and respect. 

**Responsibilities and Powers:**
- Claiming Domain and enforcing the Tradition of Domain.
- Enforcing the Six Traditions of the Camarilla.
- Granting or denying the right to create progeny (the Tradition of Progeny).
- The right of destruction (Blood Hunt).
- Settling disputes between Kindred within their domain.
