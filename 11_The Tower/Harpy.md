# Harpy

The **Harpy** is the social arbiter and scorekeeper of the Camarilla court. They do not hold physical power like the Sheriff, but they wield immense social influence. They monitor boons (the currency of favors in Kindred society) and dictate who is currently in or out of fashion.

**Responsibilities and Powers:**
- Recording and witnessing the exchange of boons and debts between Kindred.
- Publicly shaming those who default on their debts or act inappropriately.
- Defining the social status of individuals within the domain.
- Spreading gossip, rumors, and truth to manipulate the social standing of the court.
