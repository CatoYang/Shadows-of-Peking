# Seneschal

The **Seneschal** acts as the Prince's second-in-command, right hand, or chief advisor. In many ways, the Seneschal handles the day-to-day bureaucracy and minor disputes of the court, leaving the Prince free to deal with larger political matters.

**Responsibilities and Powers:**
- Stepping in to rule if the Prince is absent, incapacitated, or steps down.
- Managing the Prince's schedule and filtering petitioners.
- Overseeing the execution of the Prince's edicts.
- Often serving as a mediator before issues are brought to the Prince.
