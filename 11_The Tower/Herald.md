# Herald

The **Herald** is the voice of the Prince. They are the official messenger and announcer for the court, ensuring that the Prince's words reach all members of the domain, regardless of their status or location.

**Responsibilities and Powers:**
- Announcing the arrival of the Prince and other dignitaries at court.
- Reading edicts, laws, and declarations to the gathered Kindred.
- Serving as an official diplomatic envoy to other domains or factions.
- Maintaining the formal records and history of the Prince's reign.
