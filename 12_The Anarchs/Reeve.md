# Reeve

The **Reeve** is the Anarch equivalent of the Camarilla Sheriff. They are tasked with maintaining order, enforcing the Baron's rules, and protecting the Masquerade. However, unlike a Sheriff who enforces the will of a Prince, a Reeve is often expected to serve the community and prevent Anarchs from destroying each other.

**Responsibilities and Powers:**
- Investigating breaches of the Masquerade and cleaning up the evidence.
- Apprehending Anarchs who break the local rules or threaten the safety of the domain.
- Acting as a peacekeeper to break up fights between rival gangs.
- Executing punishments handed down by the Baron or a council of peers.
- Often viewed with suspicion by fiercely independent Anarchs who see them as "cops."
