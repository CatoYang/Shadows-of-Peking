# Warlord

A **Warlord** is a military commander within the Anarch Movement. While a Baron handles the politics and a Reeve handles the policing, a Warlord is appointed specifically to wage war. They are prominent during times of open conflict, such as a siege by the Sabbat or a crusade against the Camarilla.

**Responsibilities and Powers:**
- Organizing Anarch gangs and coteries into an effective fighting force.
- Planning and executing military campaigns, raids, and defenses.
- Training younger Anarchs in combat and survival tactics.
- Maintaining arsenals and securing weapons for the movement.
- Often, their authority supersedes the Baron's on the battlefield, though they are expected to step down during peacetime.
