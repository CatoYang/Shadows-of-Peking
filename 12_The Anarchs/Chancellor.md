# Chancellor

The **Chancellor** is the administrative and legal mind of an Anarch domain. While Anarchs detest the stifling bureaucracy of the Camarilla, they still need someone to handle the complex logistics of running a criminal empire or a supernatural commune.

**Responsibilities and Powers:**
- Advising the Baron on matters of "Anarch Law" and precedent.
- Managing the domain's financial assets, front companies, and mortal political influence.
- Serving as a neutral arbiter in disputes when the Baron is unavailable or unwilling to intervene.
- Drafting treaties and agreements with other factions.
