# Emissary

An **Emissary** is the Anarch equivalent of a diplomat or ambassador. Because Anarch domains are often decentralized and surrounded by hostile factions, communication is vital. Emissaries are chosen for their silver tongues and ability to survive walking into the lion's den.

**Responsibilities and Powers:**
- Representing the Baron or the local Anarch movement to other domains, including Camarilla Princes or neighboring Anarch Barons.
- Negotiating treaties, ceasefires, or trade agreements.
- Gathering intelligence while abroad under the guise of diplomacy.
- Delivering ultimatums or declarations of war without (hopefully) being killed on the spot.
