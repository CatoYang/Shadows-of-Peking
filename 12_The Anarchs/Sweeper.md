# Sweeper

A **Sweeper** acts as a census taker, border patrol, and information broker for the Anarch domain. In a society that values freedom of movement but fears infiltration, the Sweeper's job is to know exactly who is in the city at any given time.

**Responsibilities and Powers:**
- Monitoring the borders and transportation hubs for newly arrived Kindred.
- Interviewing newcomers to ascertain their Clan, affiliation, and intentions.
- Maintaining records (mental or otherwise) of the domain's population.
- Reporting suspicious individuals or potential spies to the Baron or the Reeve.
- Often the first point of contact for a vampire entering an Anarch city.
