# Baron

The **Baron** is the most common title for the leader of an Anarch domain. Unlike a Camarilla Prince who claims ultimate authority by right of praxis, a Baron's power is usually derived from the consent (or at least the tolerance) of the local Anarch gangs and coteries. A Baron is often viewed more as a mob boss, a union leader, or a warlord than a monarch.

**Responsibilities and Powers:**
- Mediating disputes between different Anarch factions within the territory.
- Overseeing the protection of the domain from outside threats (Sabbat, Camarilla, or Second Inquisition).
- Establishing the "local rules" (often variations or looser interpretations of the Traditions, particularly the Masquerade).
- Collecting "taxes" or favors in exchange for protection and stability.
- Their authority is only as strong as their ability to enforce it and maintain the loyalty of their followers.
