# Anarch Roles

The Anarch Movement is inherently less structured than the Camarilla, often mocking or rejecting their rigid hierarchies. However, practicality necessitates some level of organization within a Free State or Anarch domain. The following are common roles found among the Anarchs, though titles and responsibilities can vary wildly from city to city:

- [[Baron]]
- [[Emissary]]
- [[Sweeper]]
- [[Chancellor]]
- [[Warlord]]
- [[Reeve]]
