# The Imperial Eunuchs

While not an army in the streets, the eunuchs of the Forbidden City constituted a massive, shadow-faction that controlled the flow of information and power within the Imperial Court.

## Key Figures
* **Li Lianying (The Chief Eunuch):** The favorite and most trusted confidant of Empress Dowager Cixi. He held immense, terrifying informal power. High-ranking nobles and provincial viceroys frequently had to bribe Li Lianying just to have their memorials (petitions) placed on the Empress Dowager's desk.

## The Shadow Court
* There were thousands of eunuchs serving in the Forbidden City. Because they were the only men allowed in the inner courtyards where Cixi lived and ruled, they were the ultimate gatekeepers.
* They acted as spies, intercepted foreign intelligence, and whispered directly into the ears of the royalty.
* During the Boxer crisis, the eunuchs heavily favored the Boxers, largely because the conservative, traditionalist Boxers promised to preserve the old Imperial system that gave the eunuchs their vast wealth and power, whereas the Han reformers and foreigners threatened to dismantle it.
