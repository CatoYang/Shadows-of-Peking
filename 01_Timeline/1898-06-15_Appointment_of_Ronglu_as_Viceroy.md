# Appointment of Ronglu as Viceroy (June 15, 1898)

Empress Dowager Cixi was a master of political strategy. On the exact same day she forced the Emperor to dismiss his tutor (Weng Tonghe), she made a brilliant, preemptive military countermove to ensure the reformist movement remained toothless.

## The Appointment
* Cixi forced Emperor Guangxu to appoint her most trusted confidant and loyalist, General Ronglu, as the Viceroy of Zhili (the province surrounding Peking) and the Supreme Commander of the Beiyang Armies.
* This gave Ronglu direct, absolute command over all modernized military forces in northern China, including the troops commanded by Yuan Shikai, Nie Shicheng, and Dong Fuxiang.

## The Strategic Checkmate
* The reformers had ideas, but Cixi now had all the guns. 
* By placing her loyalist in control of the military ring around the capital, Cixi guaranteed that if the Emperor or his radical reformers ever attempted a physical coup or tried to enforce an edict by military decree, they would be immediately crushed. This move directly necessitated Tan Sitong's desperate gamble with Yuan Shikai months later.
