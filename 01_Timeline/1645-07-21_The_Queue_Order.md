# The Queue Order (July 21, 1645)

To physically enforce their dominance and test the loyalty of their new Han subjects, the Manchu Qing Dynasty enacted a deeply humiliating cultural edict.

## "Keep your hair and lose your head"
* The Regent Dorgon ordered that all Han Chinese men must adopt the traditional Manchu hairstyle: shaving the front and top of the head completely bald, while growing the back out into a long, braided ponytail (the queue).
* The punishment for refusing to cut one's hair was immediate execution for treason. The chilling slogan was: *"Keep your hair and lose your head; keep your head and lose your hair."*

## Cultural Humiliation
* In traditional Han Confucian culture, hair was a gift from one's parents and cutting it was considered a severe mutilation and unfilial act. 
* Millions of Han Chinese were executed or committed suicide rather than submit to the Queue Order. For the next 260 years, the queue remained a deeply hated symbol of Han subjugation.
