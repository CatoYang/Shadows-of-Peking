# Desecration of the Chala Cemetery (June 1900)

In their zeal to cleanse the capital of all foreign influence, the Boxers targeted not just the living, but the dead.

## The Chala Cemetery
* Located just outside the western walls of Peking, the Chala Cemetery was the oldest and most prominent Christian burial ground in China. 
* It held the tombs of hundreds of foreign Catholics, most notably the legendary Jesuit missionaries Matteo Ricci and Johann Adam Schall von Bell, who had served the Ming and early Qing emperors centuries prior.

## The Desecration
* The Boxers believed that the corpses of foreigners were poisoning the *qi* of the earth and disrupting the natural Feng Shui, contributing to the drought.
* They violently sacked the cemetery. They smashed the ancient, intricately carved stele (tombstones), dug up the graves, and dragged the remains of the centuries-dead missionaries out of their coffins, burning the bones or scattering them in the streets.

## Storytelling Hook
* This is an incredible hook for a World of Darkness setting. Were there ancient Kindred resting in Torpor beneath those historic Jesuit tombs? The destruction of ancient gravesites forces vampires out of slumber and strips away their oldest havens, while sending the local Wraith population into an absolute frenzy.
