# The "Blood Edict" (Mid-September 1898)

As the political walls closed in around Emperor Guangxu just before the Wuxu Coup, he realized his life and his reforms were in mortal danger.

## The Smuggled Decree
* Realizing he was isolated, Guangxu supposedly wrote a secret decree using his own blood on a piece of white silk.
* In it, he begged Kang Youwei and the other reformers to "find a way to save me without delay."
* Lin Xu, one of the newly appointed reformist secretaries (and one of the future Six Gentlemen), managed to secretly smuggle this blood edict out of the Forbidden City and deliver it to Kang Youwei.

## Storytelling Fuel
* This is a literal "call to action" written in royal blood. In a Vampire chronicle, an artifact written in the blood of the Son of Heaven is incredibly potent (perhaps possessing Blood Magic properties or resonant power). 
* It was this desperate plea that triggered Tan Sitong's midnight visit to Yuan Shikai, acting as the catalyst for the doomed counter-coup.
