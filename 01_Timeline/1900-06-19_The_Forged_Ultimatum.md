# The Forged Foreign Ultimatum (June 19, 1900)

What finally pushed Empress Dowager Cixi over the edge to declare war on the entire world was very likely a massive piece of political disinformation.

## The Fake Note
* During a tense Grand Council meeting on June 19, the pro-Boxer faction (Prince Duan) presented Cixi with what they claimed was an official diplomatic ultimatum from the foreign powers.
* The note supposedly demanded four things, the most explosive being: **The immediate restoration of Emperor Guangxu to full power and the retirement of Empress Dowager Cixi.**

## Storytelling Fuel
* Historians widely believe this note was a complete forgery fabricated by Prince Duan and his allies to infuriate Cixi and force her to fight the foreigners. 
* It worked perfectly. Cixi was so enraged by the idea of foreigners dictating her retirement that she immediately ordered the Legations to evacuate, leading to the siege the next day.
* **Kindred Hook:** Who actually forged the note? Was it mortal ambition, or the manipulation of a Kindred faction (like the Anarchs or Russians) trying to ignite a war to break the Camarilla's grip on the city?
