# Burning of Fengtai Railway Station (May 28, 1900)

As the Boxers pushed into the province of Zhili, they began systematically targeting modern infrastructure, viewing it as the source of foreign corruption and ecological disruption.

## The Attack
* The Boxers descended upon the Fengtai Railway Station, a major transport hub located just a few miles southwest of Peking's walls.
* They burned the station to the ground, destroyed the locomotives, and tore up the tracks.

## The Consequence
* This was the most significant destruction of property near the capital to date. It sent a shockwave of panic through the Legation Quarter, as it proved the Boxers were now on Peking's doorstep.
* The destruction of the rail lines effectively cut Peking off from easy reinforcement or escape to the port city of Tianjin, trapping the diplomats and leading directly to their urgent call for the Legation Guards (who arrived just days later).
