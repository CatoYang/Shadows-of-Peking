# The Murder of Pearl Concubine (August 15, 1900)

As the Eight-Nation Alliance breached the walls of Peking, the Qing Court panicked and prepared to flee to the west. Before they left, a deeply tragic and brutal execution occurred within the walls of the Forbidden City.

## The Execution
* Consort Zhen (commonly known as the Pearl Concubine) was the favorite concubine of Emperor Guangxu. She had heavily supported his reforms in 1898 and had been imprisoned by Cixi ever since the Wuxu Coup.
* As the court prepared to flee in disguise, the Pearl Concubine allegedly begged Cixi to let the Emperor remain in Peking to negotiate with the foreign powers.
* Enraged by her defiance (and likely not wanting to drag a political prisoner across the country), Cixi ordered her eunuchs to throw the 24-year-old Pearl Concubine down a small well in the northeastern corner of the Forbidden City, drowning her.

## Storytelling Fuel
* The Emperor was forced to watch or listen as the love of his life was murdered, leaving him a completely broken man for the rest of his life.
* The "Well of the Pearl Concubine" remains a famous landmark in the Forbidden City today. In a World of Darkness setting, this is a prime location for an incredibly powerful Wraith, or perhaps she didn't drown, but was Embraced by a Nosferatu or Kuei-jin lurking in the palace cisterns?
