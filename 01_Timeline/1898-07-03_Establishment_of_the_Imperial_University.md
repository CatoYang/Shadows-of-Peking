# Establishment of the Imperial University (July 3, 1898)

As part of the drive to modernize Chinese education and create a new generation of Western-educated bureaucrats, Emperor Guangxu ordered the creation of a modern university in the capital.

## The Institution
* The Imperial University of Peking (Jingshi Daxuetang) was established to teach both traditional Chinese philosophy and modern Western sciences, mathematics, and law.
* It was a radical departure from traditional academies. The progressive reformer Liang Qichao drafted its initial charter.

## The Legacy
* Remarkably, when Empress Dowager Cixi launched her coup in September and violently reversed nearly all of the 103 reform edicts, she chose to spare the Imperial University. 
* It survived the conservative purge and the Boxer Rebellion, eventually evolving into the modern-day Peking University, the most prestigious university in China.
