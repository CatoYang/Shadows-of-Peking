# The Boxers Enter Peking (June 13, 1900)

Throughout early June, the Qing court had debated whether to suppress the Boxers or use them against the foreigners. By June 13, the Boxers entered the capital in massive numbers.

## The Night of Terror
* The Boxers immediately began a campaign of terror in the Tartar City.
* They targeted anything associated with foreign influence: churches, foreign businesses, and the homes of Chinese Christians.
* A massive fire was started which burned out of control, destroying the prosperous commercial district of Dashilar outside the Qianmen Gate.
* Hundreds of Chinese Christians were massacred in the streets, while foreign diplomats and remaining missionaries barricaded themselves inside the fortified Legation Quarter and the Beitang Catholic Cathedral.
