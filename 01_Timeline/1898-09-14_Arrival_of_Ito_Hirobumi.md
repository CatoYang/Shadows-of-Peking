# Arrival of Ito Hirobumi (September 14, 1898)

In the final, frantic days of the reform period, Kang Youwei proposed a radical and diplomatically explosive idea: hiring foreign experts to directly manage the Qing government.

## The Proposal
* Former Japanese Prime Minister Ito Hirobumi—the man who successfully modernized Japan during the Meiji Restoration—arrived in Peking for a visit.
* Kang Youwei petitioned Emperor Guangxu to hire Ito as a paramount foreign advisor to guide the reforms. Kang even floated the wildly ambitious idea of a formal federation between China, Japan, Britain, and the US.

## The Paranoia
* When Empress Dowager Cixi learned of this, she was horrified. To her and the conservative Manchu court, giving a foreigner (especially from the nation that had just humiliated them in 1895) control over Qing policy was tantamount to treason and selling out the empire. 
* This event was the immediate trigger that convinced Cixi the Emperor had gone completely rogue and had to be stopped immediately.
